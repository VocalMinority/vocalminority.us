How It Works

1

Members Vote

At the beginning of the month, members vote on a focus area.

2

Nonprofit Announced

Members then nominate nonprofits that meet our criteria: fighting for equity and justice, operating with less than a $1 million budget, and having good financial standing.

3

Grant is Made

Finally, that month’s total contribution is made to the selected nonprofit in the name of The Vocal Minority. By pooling our resources in this way, we are capable of making an outsized impact.
