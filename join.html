Join the Vocal Minority

Thank you for your support in the fight for equity and justice in America. To join, enter your email, name and pledge amount.

Your email will only be used by Vocal Minority to coordinate donations, and not be sold or shared with any third party organization. Expect only a few emails a month: once to announce the month’s selected nonprofit, a couple reminders to donate, and an email to recap the month’s impact.

Your name will not be published on our website, and when you donate to the organization, you can choose to make it anonymous.

You won’t need to provide payment information now; this will happen on Donation Day (the next one is scheduled for December 15). We’d just like to get a sense of how much people will be able to donate so that we know the collective impact our group can make on each organization. You can always change your commitment amount later.

Political Contributions

Currently, Vocal Minority is focused on making donations to nonpartisan 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations. If there’s enough interest from the community, we’ll explore expanding our efforts to funneling contributions to political campaigns and candidates. Would you be interested?
