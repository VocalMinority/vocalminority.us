FAQ

How exactly does it work?

Once you become a member, you are granted access to our community chat forum. Each month, our members discuss the different organizations we're interested in and choose one to support. All of the money we've pooled together in our fund is then donated to the organization we select.

Are my donations tax-deductible?

Yes. We're working with the Silicon Valley Community Foundation as a donor advised fund, which allows us to simplify the donation process while keeping everything tax-deductible. SVCF uses the billing information you enter through PayPal to mail you the necessary tax documents.

Vocal Minority is a committee advised fund of Silicon Valley Community Foundation (SVCF). All donations will be processed via SVCF, a 501 (c)(3) public charity registered in the United States (ID 20-5205488). SVCF will provide a formal acknowledgement letter containing tax-deduction language for your records. Contributions will be tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law.
